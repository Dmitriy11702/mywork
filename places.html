Park Sherbakova

Donetsk park Sherbakova. It is located in the Voroshilovsky district. It is bounded from the east by Universitetskaya Street, from the south by Stadionnaya Street, from the west by the Shakhtar Stadium, from the north by the Second City Pond.

Donbass Arena

The football stadium in Donetsk, built in 2009, is designed for 52,667 spectators. Formally, it is the home stadium of the Shakhtar football club. Since 2014, matches have not been held at the stadium. The stadium is located in the Park of culture and recreation.
